About Aleksandar

Dr. Aleksandar Marinkovic, Associate Professor at Department for Organic Chemistry, Faculty for Technology and Metallurgy at University of Belgrade - Current scientific record of Associate professor Aleksandar Marinkovic covers investigations of synthesis, structure and activity broad spectrum of organic compounds, including research in domains of (bio)material and nanomaterial engineering.

He submitted 40 patent applications, while up to date 12 of them have been realized. He is member in COST association action (CA17128). Dr. Marinkovic has published 166 original scientific papers in journals on SCI list. According to data in Scopus base, his publications have been cited 2011 by 1574 papers, h index equals 20.
