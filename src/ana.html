About Ana

Ana Popović is a PhD Candidate from the University of Belgrade, Faculty of Technology and Metallurgy at the Environmental Protection Department. She holds a M.Sc. degree in Environmental Engineering with her research background in the fields of wastewater treatment and valorization of secondary raw materials.

Ana possesses over 15 years of international professional experience in environmental management and sustainability. During her PhD research, Ana contributed to several national scientific projects and LignoCOST action (CA17128). Her current research interests are targeted around the synthesis, optimization and functionalization of highly efficient adsorbents based on natural polymers, such as kraft lignin, as an effective material for removing heavy metal ions, oxyanions and pharmaceuticals from wastewater. Ana was a part of the team to draft one patent application and develop ten novel lignin-based adsorbent materials in the form of functionalized highly-porous microspheres.
