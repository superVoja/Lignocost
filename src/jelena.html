About Jelena

Dr. Jelena Rusmirović, PhD in Chemical Engineering, Research associate in Military Technical Institute has been engaged in scientific research since 2012. Dr. Rusmirović's research field is associated with the synthesis of polymers from the waste and bio-renewable sources and the chemical functionalization of cellulose, lignin, tannin, silica and carbon based mico/nanomaterials using reactive coupling agents as well as the production of related composites.

Dr. Rusmirović has participated in 6 national (scientific, 4 innovation (vouchers) and economic (Belgrade City Secretariat) projects), and 1 bilateral (Serbia-France) and as management committee member in 2 COST associations (CA17128 and CA 19124). She has 5 awards (2 gold and 3 bronze medals at the inventor competitions), and 2nd prize at the national competition for the best technological innovation 2018. She is the supervisor of the one doctoral thesis and member of commission for one doctoral and master thesis. She is Secretary of the Scientific Council of the MTI. She has been reviewed original papers for the international journals: Chemical Engineering Journal, Carbohydrate Polymers, Express polymer letters, Science of Sintering.
